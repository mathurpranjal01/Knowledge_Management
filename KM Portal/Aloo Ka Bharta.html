ALOO KA BHARTA



Aloo ka bharta is an Indian style flavored mashed potatoes with a perfect blend of spices, served it several parts of North-India, Bengal and Pakistan also.
This is the first time I tried this recipe, I become a fan of this recipe, very easy and tasty dish. You just need boil some potatoes and then saute them with
fresh herbs and spices. You can have this dish in your fasting times also. In this recipe you can also add some ginger if you want, since this is a no onion
no garlic recipe, you can also add a pinch of asafoetida. Serve aloo ka bharta with pooris, parathas or phulkas. You can also serve this as a side dish with
dal-chapati like as I have serve in below pic. Serve Roti with aloo ka bharta, homemade curd, dal and fresh cucumber salad. This is my kind of comfort meal.
