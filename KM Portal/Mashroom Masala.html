Mushroom Curry



Mushroom Curry Recipe is an Indian style, very easy to make curry. Sliced mushrooms are in creamy and aromatic gravy, just so delicious. Yogurt or curd is used to make this gravy creamy. Make sure to use plain and less sour yogurt to get the best taste of the curry. It stays good for only 2 days otherwise yogurt will become sour. So finish it up within 2 days, I am sure you will because it taste just divine that you canï¿½t stop eating it. No tomatoes are used in this gravy unlike normal Punjabi gravies are made. Only onion and yogurt with some spices are needed to make this gravy. Onions are boiled first then made gravy which gives unique taste to it.
